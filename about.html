About Me

Tech has been a huge passion of mine for a long time. When I was younger, I played a lot of games (still do). In high school, I took a class to learn how to make my own game and followed up with a computer science course. That was my introduction to programming and led me to becomming a software engineer.

Although I have so much to learn in this field, I am also taking my time now to learn and think about "adult" things that I can start doing now to help myself and others to have a better future.

“A society grows great when old men plant trees whose shade they know they shall never sit in.” - Overused quote that I just learned about (Greek Proverb).

I also have some hobbies that I enjoy to work on my creative side. I used to make gaming videos, tutorials, and all sorts of vlogs. Film and photography are things I like to do and even got to work on them during college. Dancing has also been something I've been wanting to get better at and is something I hope to get more confident in.

Now that I am done with college, I look forward to working as a software engineer that continues to grow and write code that can contribute to the tech world (and world) I grew up to love.
