Dreams

by Andrew tranAugust 19, 2020

dream -  a noun
                a series of thoughts, images, and sensations occurring in a person's mind during sleep
                synonyms: REM sleep, nightmare, vision, fantasy, hallucination
                
                a different us that we only sometimes see
                what we strive to achieve, what we want to be
                when I put my head down and close my eyes
                letting my mind open like black through the skies

                I tell myself of all the possibilities beyond what's in my vision
                that is why dreams are so unique
                dreams show so much from right inside us

                we are dreamers
                we strive to make them real
                the stream starts inside every person
                waiting to be shared
                waiting for the grand reveal

               when I try to look back at my dreams
               I have a hard time remembering them
               but dreams aren't meant to be remembered
               dreams are meant to be the days you are happy
               the day you realize you have a reason
               that you survived all those dark nights
               all the changing seasons

               but dreams are never as long as they seem
